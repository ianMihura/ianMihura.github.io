Genesis 1

In the beginning created God the heavens and the earth; the earth was a formless void, and darkness covered the face of the deep, while a wind from God swept and over the face of the waters. Then God said, ‘Let there be light’; and there was light. And God saw that the light was good; and God, separated the light from the darkness. God called the light Day, and the darkness he called Night. And there was evening and there was morning, the first day.

Pfeifen des Windes

Es ragt ins Meer der Runenstein,
Da sitz’ ich mit meinen Träumen.
Es pfeift der Wind, die Möwen schrein,
Die Wellen, die wandern und schäumen.

Ich habe geliebt manch schönes Kind
Und manchen guten Gesellen–
Wo sind sie hin? Es pfeift der Wind,
Es schäumen und wandern die Wellen

Johann von Goethe–
